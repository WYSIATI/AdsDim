## English (primary)

### Name

AdsDim

### Short description (<= 132 chars)

Dim the ads, let real posts shine. Marks ads on the x.com timeline without ever hiding posts.

### Detailed description

AdsDim marks ads on your x.com (Twitter) timeline — it never hides, deletes, or
reorders a single post. Your feed stays complete: genuine posts shine brighter
while ads quietly recede.

Mark, never hide

- Every post stays in your timeline. Ads get a small "Ad", "Sponsored", or
  "Possible" pill and visually recede — hover any dimmed post to restore it to
  full visibility instantly.
- Zero layout shift: the timeline never jumps, and AdsDim is safe with X's
  virtualized scrolling.

Two-tier detection, fully local

- Hard ads: X's own promoted/ad badge, recognized across 12 display languages.
- Possible ads: conservative heuristics for undisclosed promotions — #ad-style
  hashtags, discount codes, affiliate links, contact-to-buy and giveaway
  patterns. Each "Possible" mark comes with a why-tooltip explaining exactly
  which signal triggered it, so you can judge for yourself.

Three looks, two contrast levels

- Glass Focus (default): genuine posts float as frosted-glass cards while ads
  recede, desaturated.
- Glow Lane: genuine posts earn a gradient ring and a soft left glow; ads fade
  toward gray.
- Theater: ads dim like house lights going down; genuine posts stay at full
  brightness.
- Each scheme offers Normal and Strong contrast, and follows X's dark/light
  theme automatically.

Private by design

- Zero network calls: all detection runs locally in your browser.
- Nothing is collected, uploaded, or tracked — no analytics, no telemetry.
- Your settings are saved only in Chrome's extension sync storage.
- Open source under Apache-2.0.

### Single-purpose statement

AdsDim has a single purpose: visually marking and dimming advertising posts on
the x.com / twitter.com timeline, without hiding or removing any content.

### Permission justifications

- `storage`: saves the user's appearance settings (scheme, contrast, language)
  in Chrome extension sync storage. No other data is stored.
- Host access `x.com`, `twitter.com`, `mobile.twitter.com`: the extension's
  sole product surface. The content script must run on the X timeline to
  detect ad badges and apply the visual marking styles. No page data leaves
  the browser.

### Data usage disclosure (CWS privacy tab)

- Does not collect or transmit any user data.
- No analytics or third-party code.
- All processing happens locally in the browser.

## 简体中文 (secondary)

### 名称

AdsDim

### 简短描述

调暗广告，让真实内容更醒目。仅标记 x.com 时间线上的广告，绝不隐藏任何帖子。

### 详细描述

AdsDim 会标记你 x.com（Twitter）时间线上的广告——绝不隐藏、删除或重排任何一条
帖子。你的时间线保持完整：真实内容更加醒目，广告安静地退后。

只标记，不隐藏

- 每条帖子都保留在时间线中。广告会带上"广告 / Sponsored / 疑似"小标签并在视觉上
  退后——将鼠标悬停在任何变暗的帖子上即可立刻恢复完整显示。
- 零布局偏移：时间线不会跳动，并且与 X 的虚拟化滚动完全兼容。

两级检测，全部本地完成

- 硬广告：识别 X 官方的推广标记，支持 12 种显示语言。
- 疑似广告：针对未披露推广的保守启发式检测——#ad 类话题标签、优惠码、联盟链接、
  私信购买与抽奖套路等。每个"疑似"标记都附带原因提示，说明触发了哪条规则，由你
  自行判断。

三种视觉方案 × 两档对比度

- 玻璃聚焦（默认）：真实帖子如磨砂玻璃卡片般浮起，广告退后并去饱和。
- 光晕通道：真实帖子获得渐变描边与左侧光晕，广告向灰色淡去。
- 剧场模式：广告像剧场熄灯般变暗，真实帖子保持全亮。
- 每种方案均有普通/加强两档对比度，并自动跟随 X 的深浅色主题。

隐私优先

- 零网络请求：所有检测都在你的浏览器本地运行。
- 不收集、不上传、不追踪——没有任何分析或遥测代码。
- 设置仅保存在 Chrome 扩展同步存储中。
- 基于 Apache-2.0 协议开源。

### 单一用途声明

AdsDim 只有一个用途：在 x.com / twitter.com 时间线上以视觉方式标记并调暗广告
帖子，不隐藏、不删除任何内容。

### 权限说明

- `storage`：在 Chrome 扩展同步存储中保存用户的外观设置（方案、对比度、语言），
  不存储任何其他数据。
- 主机权限 `x.com`、`twitter.com`、`mobile.twitter.com`：扩展唯一的作用页面。
  内容脚本必须在 X 时间线上运行才能检测广告标记并应用视觉样式。任何页面数据都
  不会离开浏览器。
